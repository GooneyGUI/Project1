OUR MISSION
Keeping Akron Healthy One Dance at a Time

Founded in August 2012

We are a "Just" Cause Organization created to promote healthy living and awareness through the exciting avenue of line dancing!

JustUs Line Dance Crew promotes the continued growth and fun of line dancing!

We aim to bring a refreshed and traditional style of line dancing through members seasoned and youthful.

As an added bonus we present an exciting and fun atmosphere with a weekly class and a work-out class.
